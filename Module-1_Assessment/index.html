About Me

Hello! I know the picture of me is small but it is the most recent one I have of myself. I think it's pretty easy to see that I like to travel on a motorcyle. The current picture was went I went to Mohave National Preserve. Some other places I've been have been the Badlands, Death Valley, Glacier National Park, Canada, and even a small bit of Alaska.

I have recently graduated St. Olaf College with degrees in both Physics and Math. I have always liked to figure out how the world works. I wasn't originally going to have a double major but that changed during college. The math degree came about after I studied abroad in Budapest with some friends. After that, we just kept taking more and more math classes together until we all had math degrees.

One of the things that made me pursue physics in the first place was fluid dynamics, but mainly water. This is probably due to how much time I spent in it as a kid. This usually entailed swimming and sailing, both of which I still do. I have been a competative swimmer for around 9 years now and still find the water fascinating. The sailing is something I enjoy with my cousins. We recently purchased a boat together that can fit all of us, so whenever we are at our grandmother's cabin we can sail all day.
